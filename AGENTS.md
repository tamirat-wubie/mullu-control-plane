## Universal Action Orchestration

Every effect-bearing action must pass through Universal Action Orchestration before execution.

```text
UAO(action, state, memory, policy, capabilities, temporal_rules, exposure_boundary)
  -> <state_prime, memory_prime, causal_decision_trace, decision, receipts, observed_outcome, deltas>
```

No-bypass rule:

```text
effect_bearing(action) and not passed_through_UAO(action)
```

a manual approval checkpoint unless the action crosses a hard boundary or the
active policy explicitly requires operator review.
