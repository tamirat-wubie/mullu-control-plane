## Mission

Mullusi exists to make powerful AI systems safe enough to trust.

Mullu Govern makes AI action governable, auditable, and accountable.

As AI moves from answering questions to taking actions, organizations need control, proof, approval, budget limits, audit trails, rollback paths, and clear responsibility.

## Problem

AI systems are becoming capable of acting across tools, files, workflows, messages, budgets, and business processes. That creates a trust gap: people need to know what happened, why it happened, who approved it, what evidence supported it, and how to recover when a boundary is crossed.

## Solution

Mullu Govern gives AI action a control plane: policy checks, approval gates, budget limits, evidence, receipts, audit trails, and rollback paths.

The goal is accountable automation: useful AI action that can be limited, reviewed, approved, blocked, and audited.

## Market Role

Mullu Govern is not mainly a model, chatbot, or creative assistant. It is a trust layer for AI actions.

It helps the market move from unchecked automation to governed work.

## Advanced-AI Fear Response

Mullu Govern is not built to unleash unchecked machine autonomy. It is built to limit, verify, and govern AI-driven action with human approval, policy checks, budget boundaries, evidence, receipts, and rollback paths.
